HOW TO BEAT THAT FACE

Step 1:Cleanse your face

It imperative to hav a clean base, so make sure you wash your face thoroughly before starting your makeup routine.

Step 2:Prime your face

Prep your face for a solid foundation. Primer helps with making your makeup last longer and reducing shine.This step is crcuial if you have oily skin.

Step 3:Best eyes

Whether you want a subtle makeup look or a more in your face look, choosing the right eyeshadow and blending it well is key.

Step 4:Build that foundation

This is the most important part of your makeup routine. It is imperative that you use a makeup that matches your skin colour and skin type. If you have oily skin, choose a matifying foundation.

Step 5:Conceal and Contourthem assets

Concealing the high points of your face such as under eyes and cheeks, forehead and nose.Contour adds definition to your face.

Step 6:Blush,Bronze,Highlight

Adding some coulour to your face with blush and bronzer and a little shimmer from a highlighter can take your look to the next level.

Step 7:Smooches

Complete the look with lipstick whether red, nude, or just some gloss, but ensure those lips are lined.

Step 8:All Set

Finish the look with a setting spray to hold everything in place for longer.
