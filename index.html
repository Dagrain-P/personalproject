Embrace the BBW in you

What it means to be BBW

Beautiful

Being beautiful is not just what is on the outside. Taking care of yourself can be quite rewarding. From eating healthy,working out and meditating it requires effort and consistency. A beautiful mind is important for outward beauty. Being beautiful means you exude confidence and have great aura.

Black

Being black means loving the skin you were born in. Embracing your hair, hips, curves and honring your ancestors. It is important for black people to familiarize themself with the past and use it as a guide. Know why our culture is so unique.

Woman

Women often don't have access to same resources and given the same opprtunities as men. But with perseverance many women have achieved the unthinkable and so can you! Be empowered to achieve all your dreams and break barriers. You are a queen and can achieve success too.
